Historical Origins

Bian Que Learned from Chang Sang Jun

According to the 'Records of the Grand Historian', Bian Que served as an inn manager in his youth. Chang Sang Jun, an eccentric with profound medical skills, frequented the inn. Bian Que noticed his extraordinary nature and treated him with great respect.

Moved by his sincerity, Chang Sang Jun observed Bian Que for over a decade before deciding to pass on his secret formulas. He gave Bian Que a miraculous prescription that, after thirty days, enabled him to 'see through walls', mastering the pulse theory and becoming a legendary physician.

Academic Lineage: Discourse on the Normal Pulse

The core theory of the Chang Sang Jun Pulse Method is deeply rooted in classics like the 'Huangdi Neijing', specifically the 'Discourse on the Normal Pulse'. It details the normal pulse variations across different climates and seasons.

This method inherits and expands on this ancient theory, emphasizing the 'pulse-breath harmony'. By assessing the ratio between pulse and respiration, it accurately evaluates the body's Qi and blood, representing a precious living lineage of ancient pulse science.

Contemporary Inheritance: Prof. Li Shusen

The Chang Sang Jun Pulse Method was once hidden among the folk. Today, thanks to Professor Li Shusen's thirty years of dedicated research and selfless sharing, this ultimate medical art has regained its glory.
